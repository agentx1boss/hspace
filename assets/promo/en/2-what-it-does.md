# What it does

The draft your AI wrote — a web demo, a Markdown proposal, a report — you want it to reach a client, a teammate, a small circle, not the whole internet.

Regular hosts race to "publish to the world." HSpace goes the other way: **targeted sharing**. One line to remember it —

> **Ship to one, not to all.**

## Three things

- 🚀 **Out in seconds**: one click from your editor or an AI chat; link + password in a single paste; zero signup, zero config.
- 🎯 **You stay in the know**: receipts on who opened it and how often; change the password to revoke; per-recipient links — kick one person out without changing everyone else's.
- 🔁 **The draft changes, the link doesn't**: iterate freely, roll back to any version, no more "final_v3" links.

## Also

| Capability | What it means |
